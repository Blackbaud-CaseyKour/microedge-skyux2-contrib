Microedge SKY UX Contrib Library

A holding place for Microedge SKY UX components that are yet contributed to the main SKY UX repository.

SKY UX is a new version of Blackbaud's user experience framework. The UX framework brings a consistent, cohesive experience to our products and allows third-party developers to provide the same consistent experience in their customizations and applications. SKY UX takes advantage of Angular to keep SKY UX modern while increasing the tooling, testing, and performance available.
